Style and Sector ETF Rotation with bt

Rendered notebook transcript. This page is generated from examples/notebooks/quant_trading/06_style_sector_asset_rotation_bt.ipynb and includes code cells plus captured outputs from the committed notebook.

ETF rotation is a natural target-weight problem. This notebook constructs De-Time rotation weights and shows how they can be passed to the bt framework.
